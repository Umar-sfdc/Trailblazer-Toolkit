What is Trigger?

Trigger is a pieace of code that is executed before and after a record is update or inserted.


There are two types of trigger in Salesforce.

1. Before Trigger: These trigger are fired before the data is saved into the database.
2. After Trigger: These trigger are fired after the data is saved into the database.


Trigger syntax:

Trigger <Trigger Name> On <Object Name> (<trigger events>){

// logic to be implemented.

}
